VapeCraft is a Minecraft mod I am working on that allows players to build their own juuls.

Version 0.4.0

Features I have now: create juul, juul pods, and take fatty rips. You can also mine nic salt from the
ground. You are also addicted and need to Juul at least every 20 seconds or you will literally die.
I want to add tobacco farms and more complex crafting recipes.
